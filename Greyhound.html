Greyhound

Key Facts

  • Country of Origin: Egypt/Great Britain
  • Date of Origin: Antiquity
  • First Use: Large game coursing
  • Use Today: Racing, coursing, companion
  • Life Expectancy: 10-12 years
  • Breed Size: Large
  • Weight Range: 60-70 lbs
  • Related Breeds: Spanish Greyhound, Italian Greyhound, Rampur Greyhound
Capable of reaching 43 mph, the elegant Greyhound is the dog world's most important speed merchant. This surprisingly gentle breed uses speed and sight to overtake prey, be it a living animal in the field or desert, or a mechanical rabbit on a dog track. The pet Greyhound is a delightful and relaxed companion, although dogs that have retired from racing have a tendency to chase anything that moves.

Breed Temperament Facts: Poor with children. Poor in urban environment. Good in warm climates. Requires minimal grooming. Needs considerable exercise.
